01 About
AD ASTRA PER ASPERA To the stars through difficulties.

I was born in Lagos, Nigeria. I have lived in five countries and traveled to 36 so far. I am a Manchester United fan for life. I love my family and friends. That is the part of my life that feels most real.

I am naturally curious. I have always liked taking things apart to understand how they work. That instinct shows up everywhere. In football, I watch how the game opens up before a goal. In business, I look at incentives and second-order effects. In technology, I pay attention to the small decisions that quietly compound.

Today, I build AI systems and invest in African and emerging markets. I am less interested in hype and more interested in durability. What survives pressure. What breaks when assumptions are wrong. What actually lasts.

I travel because I like seeing how people live. I read because it slows me down. I care about building things that matter, but I care just as much about staying grounded while doing it.
